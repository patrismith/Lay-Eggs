Controls:

  • Space bar: lay an egg or perform a mating call
  • Click the canvas: direct your circle to move to that location
  • Click and drag on the canvas: your circle follows the cursor

This is a simulation of the Circle species. You will control various Circles; the one you currently control will be surrounded with a halo.

There are female and male circles. The females are quicker and smaller, and the males are larger and slower.

The female can lay a limited number of eggs in her lifetime. Once she lays them all, she gets no more. The female must become fertilized first, by passing through a male performing the mating call.

The male can perform the mating call but once in his lifetime. It looks like the outline of a circle radiating from its center.

Squares begin to invade from offscreen. If a circle touches one, they both are destroyed. Without your help, the squares will eventually destroy all the circles.

By strategizing, can you get the population to 100?
