Version with audio

Controls:

  • Space bar or Click bottom right corner: lay an egg or perform a mating call
  • Click the canvas: direct your circle to move to that location
  • Click and drag on the canvas: direct your circle to follow the cursor

This is a simulation of the Circle species. You will start the game controlling one of these creatures. Your goal is to increase the population to 100 individuals.

Player Circle

The Circle you currently control is surrounded by a yellow halo. Move your Circle around the screen by clicking on the spot where you'd like it to go. You can also click-and-drag to have your Circle follow your mouse cursor.

If your Circle dies, control will switch to another Circle. Look for the yellow halo to identify it.

Circle Biology

Circles have a limited lifespan. If they survive all other hazards, they will eventually die of old age.

When Circles are born, they are very pale. Circles can reproduce once their color has darkened. There are female and male Circles. You can identify them by their size and behavior. The females are quicker and smaller. The males are larger and slower.

The female Circle can lay eggs that hatch into baby Circles. In order to lay them, she must first be fertilized by a male Circle while he performs the mating call. This is accomplished by colliding or passing through the male Circle's body.

The male can perform the mating call but once in his lifetime. It looks like an outline radiating from its center. Any female Circle of sufficient age who passes within him will become fertile, and able to lay her eggs.

If you are playing as a female Circle, press the space bar or tap the bottom right corner to lay an egg. If you are playing as a male Circle, press the space bar to perform the mating call. Warning! An individual Circle only gets one mating call or five eggs for the duration of their existence.

Enemies of the Circle

Squares will begin to invade from offscreen. If a circle touches one, they both are destroyed.

Without your help, the squares will eventually destroy all the circles. By strategizing, can you get the population to 100?
